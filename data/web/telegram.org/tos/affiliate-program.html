2. Scope

2.1. Creating Affiliate Programs

Users can create an Affiliate Program for TPA they own, establishing a set commission period for the program and a specific commission percentage for Affiliates. The commission period is defined as the length of time, starting from the date a user joins a TPA through an Affiliate, during which said Affiliate earns commissions from any transactions made by users they refer. The commission percentage is defined as the percentage of Star proceeds from referred users within a TPA earned by the Affiliate who referred them to said TPA. For clarity, the commission period does not affect the time frame available for potential Affiliates to join a program from the moment it is created; instead, all programs remain accessible indefinitely unless manually terminated by their creator.

Channel owners, Telegram users, as well as owners of other TPA are able to join the program as Affiliates, receiving a unique referral link that they can share with other Telegram users.
When a user makes a purchase with Telegram Stars inside a TPA that they originally discovered via a referral link, a percentage of the Stars they spend in the TPA is deducted and sent to the referring party (Affiliate), in accordance with the commission percentage and commission period chosen by the Program Creator when establishing their affiliate program.

Once an affiliate program is initiated, its commission rate and commission period cannot be decreased. Should a Program Creator wish to amend the details of their program, they will only be able to prolong its commission period, increase its commission rate or terminate the program entirely. In the event of manual termination of the program, all referral links released thereunder will be disabled shortly thereafter, but existing affiliates will continue to receive commissions for transactions made by users they previously referred until the originally established commission period has elapsed..

Channel owners, Telegram users and TPA developers are able to opt-in to active affiliate programs, receiving a unique link to share with their audiences. If a user opens an affiliate link and then goes on to make a purchase of digital goods or services in the respective TPA, a portion of the Stars used for the purchase will in turn be sent to the Affiliate's balance, in accordance with the commission rate set by the Program Creator.
